Ingredients

  • 1/2 pound ground pork
  • 1/2 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 diced onion
  • Canned tomatoes
  • 2 tblsp of parsley
  • 1 clove of garlic, crushed
  • Dash of sugar
  • Dried basil
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt to taste
  • Black pepper
  • Noodles
  • Cottage cheese
  • Parmesan
  • Shredded mozzarella
  • Eggs

Steps

  1. Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar.
  2. Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente.
  3. Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl.
  4. Meanwhile, make the sauce.
  5. Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled.
  6. Cover with foil and bake in the preheated oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown.
